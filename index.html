Have A Listen

A nonprofit organisation for the Development and Popularisation of Swiss Music

Who we are

Yvan Bing

Alex Duloz

Yvan and Alex are passionate about the Swiss music scene and its awesome bands, and both of them are active contributors to that scene as producers, composers, artists, and sound engineers. Yvan runs Kitchen Studio, a Geneva-based studio known by many Swiss and international artists for its professionalism and the quality of its work, while Alex runs the Panaviscope project, for which he plays all the instrumentals, sings, and creates the visuals.

Yvan and Alex are both connected to many bands in their country, with whom they talk, work, and exchange ideas. Their love for this incredible pool of talent is what led them to create “Have A Listen”, a venture that aims to build links between different genres and projects while at the same taking Swiss music to places where it’s never been heard.

Our projects

The goal of “Have A Listen” is to foster interaction between Swiss musicians and increase the exposure of Swiss music, both within Switzerland and internationally. We strive to adopt a creative and artistic approach to our roles as musicians and producers.

New contexts

The “new contexts” project aims at bringing Swiss music to places where it’s never been played before. Malls, shops, bars and restaurants, stadiums, holding music—you name it. We are actively working to contact people with suggestions for Swiss playlists to be used in these settings to increase the profile of the Swiss music scene.

Words and quotes

In January 2021, we’ll be posting a new quote by a Swiss artist every day. Over the month of December 2020, we sent a series of questions to Swiss musicians and from their answers we extracted quotes about their sources of inspiration, their tastes, their creative drives, and their vision for Swiss music

Collaborations and featuring

Getting bands to work together and collaborate with one another is a passion for both Yvan and Alex. We are currently working to contact musicians with the ultimate aim of bringing them together to produce a brand-new song that will blend their own unique sounds. We hope that this will encourage a spirit of collaboration and give them a sense of being part of the same scene.
